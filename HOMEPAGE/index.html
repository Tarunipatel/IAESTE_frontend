History Of IAESTE

In 1948, A new desire for international understanding and co-operation spread around the world. 10 European countries met led by Mr James Newby set up an international exchange program for welfare of youngsters. Students with international experience are believed to be the key in tackling today's global challenges.

One of It's Kind

To reach out to as many individuals as possible and provide them the opportunities to grow their proffesional lives and experience cultures of different countries to enable international cooperation.

A Vast Network

IAESTE is present in over 80+ countries around the world, which gives you an opportunity to work all around the world.
